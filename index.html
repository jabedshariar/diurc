Welcome to the DIU Robotics Club

The DIU Robotics Club is a vibrant and dynamic student organization at Daffodil International University, dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of robotics, electronics, and intelligent systems. As one of the most active and innovative clubs on campus, we create opportunities for students from all departments to learn, build, and showcase their skills in robotics and automation. Through workshops, competitions, and collaborative projects, the club serves as a platform for transforming creative ideas into real-world solutions, while fostering teamwork, problem-solving, and leadership among its members.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and empower students by providing hands-on experiences in robotics and related technologies. We aim to bridge the gap between classroom knowledge and practical application, encourage innovation, and promote interdisciplinary collaboration. By nurturing talent and curiosity, the DIU Robotics Club seeks to prepare the next generation of engineers, innovators, and leaders who will contribute to the advancement of technology and society.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every student has the opportunity to explore and excel in robotics and emerging technologies. By becoming a hub of creativity, innovation, and technical excellence, the DIU Robotics Club aspires to place Daffodil International University on the global map of robotics and research. Our long-term vision is to inspire sustainable technological development and create future leaders capable of solving real-world challenges through robotics and automation.

Our Key Projects

Highlighting our latest innovations in automation, competition, and research.

Rescue Rover v3.0

A semi-autonomous robot designed for search and rescue operations in simulated disaster zones. Features real-time environmental data collection and obstacle avoidance.

Status: Under Development | Team: Alpha-Tech

AI-Powered Drone Navigation

Integration of computer vision and machine learning models for precision navigation and payload delivery using a custom-built quadcopter platform.

Status: Completed | Team: Visionaries

Smart Home Automation System

A prototype system using IoT and custom microcontrollers to manage lighting, climate, and security within a model home environment.

Status: Completed | Team: Electro-Core

Club Achievements & Recognition

A history of our success in local and international competitions.

2023 - A Year of Triumphs

  • **National Robotics Challenge:** 1st Place in Line Following and 3rd Place in Soccer Robot categories (Dhaka, 2023).
  • **IEEE Xtreme Programming Contest:** Top 10 finish globally (Virtual, 2023).
  • **Best Student Club Award:** Recognized by the DIU Student Affairs Office for the 3rd consecutive year.

2022 Highlights

  • **International Robo-Fest:** Finalists in the Drone Race category (Nepal, 2022).
  • **Innovation Expo:** Two projects selected for national funding support.
